Ocean Recycling

Protecting our oceans and giving plastic a second life

Parley(our ispiration)

Removing plastic waste from oceans helps restore marine ecosystems and protect wildlife,we got the idea from Parley reflected on the importance of cleaning the sea, so we decided to make this toy truck that is made all with plastic, and the plastic is the plastic recycled from sea.

Body of truck

It is the main part of our product and it is the "body" part that you put every components on it

The Wheel

We built our wheel with a circle extruded and made another circle cut inside to make it more realistic, then we made a circular space (5mm diamater) for the axis, and last we used fillet to let it be suitable for children.

The Axis

The Axis was the simplest thing we did, we made the circle with 4.95mm,and extruded it

The Container

The container is the part which you put some elements inside and transport them using the car. it can bring

The closer for the container

We built this because we need to put the container on the car body and not let it fall, It should be put inside the cylinder space on the truck to close the axis for the container.

Community beach cleanups remove waste before it reaches the ocean.
